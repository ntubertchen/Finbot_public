Language Undestanding

06.24.2017

Here we first define, to understand a sentence is to know the basic intention of it and to label the information it contains. That is what we call intent detection and slot filling.

Multi turn Language Understanding based on previous turn’s slot label, intent label and system action. In this case we not only feed current frame’s natural language but also concatenate with previous turn’s information. Since it’s often correlate with previous information. We found it important to add this into our model.

The fundamental model is a bi-directional RNN that is usually used in SLU. Each words may or may not contain information that would contribute to language understanding. So it is important that we make prediction after seeing all the nearby words in both directions.

Secondly, after know the intention and label the related information. We have to transform into the form that we can use, that is, the api format. Even though to understand dates, stock name or even currency names is relatively an easy work, how people convey these information, on the other hand, may be a tricky issue.

In real user experience, for example, people often type country name rather than its currencies code name. There are two issues to deal with in this problem. First, one country may use more than one currencies, there is ambiguous exist inside this sentence and need further confirmation. Second, we need to build the rule based transformation inside. This actually may become a potential issue in future work. That is we don’t know that it’s actually meaning is that country or is currency. To create a name slot label and let the model to decide is the best policy I think.
